Strategic Development Initiatives by OPRDF

Employment

Oggy Yuva Vikas Yojna (OYVY)

A direct, industry-linked training and stipend initiative designed to bridge the gap between technical education and guaranteed corporate placement.

  • Monthly Stipend: ₹8,000 during training phase.
  • Skill Alignment: Core IT, advanced manufacturing, or green tech.
  • Direct Benefit: Assured 100% placement upon certification.

Healthcare

Universal Suraksha Health Card

A sovereign health coverage mandate enabling completely cashless tertiary medical treatments and diagnostics at any district center.

  • Coverage Capital: Up to ₹5 Lakhs per family annually.
  • Zero Cost Diagnostics: Free MRI, CT scans, and lab testing.
  • Medicines: 100% subsidized essential pharmacy access.
